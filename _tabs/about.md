## whoami
Information and Cyber Security student with a habit of poking at things until they break.
This blog is where I document my learning process. CTF writeups, exploit notes, and the occasional rabbit hole I fell down at 2am. It's part learning journal, part reference for future me, and hopefully useful to anyone else working through the same problems.

## what you'll find here
- **Binary exploitation**: stack overflows, format strings, ROP chains
- **CTF writeups**: mostly pwn and reversing, or whatever else I'm grinding that week
- **Exploit education**: breaking down the why behind techniques, not just the steps
